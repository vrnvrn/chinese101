June 9:
Telling Time & Daily Routine

Words 126–150 · Clock words and the verbs of an ordinary day

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson…

  • 1Read a clock time: 点 for the hour, 分 for the minutes
  • 2Tell 现在 (now) from 时候 (a moment) and 时间 (time in general)
  • 3Walk through a daily routine: 起床, 睡觉, 吃, 喝, 休息
  • 4Use the paired sense verbs: 看 / 看见 and 听 / 听见
  • 5Say what you 说, 读, and 写: speak, read, and write
  • 6Mark something as 早 (early) or 晚 (late)
