Tip: when ordering at a Malaysian kopitiam (coffee shop), you can often just point and say 我要一份这个。 Wǒ yào yī fèn zhège. "I want one of this."

03
Key Phrases
What to say at every stage of the meal
Essential Expressions

Phrases for Every Situation

Getting a Table
Waiter
请问，有几位？
Qǐngwèn, yǒu jǐ wèi?
Excuse me, how many people?
You
我们有两位。
Wǒmen yǒu liǎng wèi.
We have two people.
Waiter
好的，请跟我来。
Hǎo de, qǐng gēn wǒ lái.
Sure, please follow me.
Calling the Waiter and Ordering
You
服务员！
Fúwùyuán!
Waiter! (to get attention)
You
我要点菜。
Wǒ yào diǎn cài.
I want to order.
You
我要一杯热茶。
Wǒ yào yī bēi rè chá.
I want a cup of hot tea.
You
我要一份炒饭。
Wǒ yào yī fèn chǎofàn.
I want one fried rice.
Dietary Needs
You
我是素食者。
Wǒ shì sùshízhě.
I am vegetarian.
You
有没有素食菜？
Yǒu méiyǒu sùshí cài?
Is there vegetarian food?
You
我不吃肉。
Wǒ bù chī ròu.
I do not eat meat.
You
不要辣，谢谢。
Bù yào là, xièxie.
No spice please, thank you.
During and After the Meal
You
这个好吃！
Zhège hǎochī!
This is delicious!
You
很好吃，谢谢。
Hěn hǎochī, xièxie.
Very delicious, thank you.
You
我吃饱了。
Wǒ chī bǎo le.
I am full.
Paying the Bill
You
买单！
Mǎidān!
Check please! (most common)
You
一共多少钱？
Yīgòng duōshao qián?
What is the total?
You
这个多少钱？
Zhège duōshao qián?
How much is this?
