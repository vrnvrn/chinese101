Grammar

有 — to have.

有 (yǒu) expresses possession and existence. Its negative is 没有 (méiyǒu) — never 不有.

有
yǒu
to have · there is
没有
méiyǒu
to not have · there is not
I have ___
我有一个妹妹。
Wǒ yǒu yī gè mèimei.
I have one younger sister.
Do you have ___?
你有哥哥吗？
Nǐ yǒu gēge ma?
Do you have an older brother?
I don't have ___
我没有哥哥。
Wǒ méiyǒu gēge.
I don't have an older brother.

Remember: The negative of 有 is always 没有 — never 不有. This is one of the few cases where 不 doesn't work.

02
Measure Words 量词
The hidden layer between numbers and nouns
量词 Measure Words

Numbers need a bridge.

In Chinese, you cannot put a number directly before a noun. You need a measure word: number + measure word + noun. English has these too: "a sheet of paper", "a head of cattle."

个
gè (neutral tone)
General-purpose measure word. Use for people and most objects when in doubt.
三个人 sān gè rén — three people  ·  一个朋友 yī gè péngyou — one friend
本
běn (3rd tone)
For books, notebooks, and bound volumes.
两本书 liǎng běn shū — two books  ·  一本杂志 yī běn zázhì — one magazine
张
zhāng (1st tone)
For flat, sheet-like objects: paper, photos, tickets, tables.
一张纸 yī zhāng zhǐ — one sheet of paper  ·  两张票 liǎng zhāng piào — two tickets

Tip: When counting two things, use 两 (liǎng) instead of 二 (èr). So it's 两个人, not 二个人.

Patterns

Number + 个 + noun. Say them all.

How many siblings do you have?
你有几个兄弟姐妹？
Nǐ yǒu jǐ gè xiōngdì jiěmèi?
How many siblings do you have?
I have two younger sisters
我有两个妹妹。
Wǒ yǒu liǎng gè mèimei.
I have two younger sisters.
I have three books
我有三本书。
Wǒ yǒu sān běn shū.
I have three books.
I'm an only child
我是独生子 / 独生女。
Wǒ shì dúshēngzǐ / dúshēngnǚ.
I am an only child. (son / daughter)

几 (jǐ) = "how many?" for small numbers (under 10). For larger numbers, use 多少 (duōshao).

Days of the Week 星期

Seven days. One word each.

星期 (xīngqī) means "week." Just add the number 1–6 for Monday–Saturday. Sunday is the exception: 星期天 or 星期日.

Dates 日期

Year → Month → Day.

Chinese dates go from big to small: year first, then month, then day — the opposite of American format and the same as ISO format.

年
nián
year
月
yuè
month
号/日
hào / rì
day (of month)
Today's date
今天是二○二六年三月十九号。
Jīntiān shì èr líng èr liù nián sān yuè shíjiǔ hào.
Today is March 19th, 2026.
What's today's date?
今天几月几号？
Jīntiān jǐ yuè jǐ hào?
What month and day is today?

Months are just numbers: 一月 = January, 二月 = February … 十二月 = December. No need to memorize twelve separate names.

Key Patterns

Asking about time and plans.

What day is today?
今天是星期几？
Jīntiān shì xīngqī jǐ?
What day of the week is today?
Are you free tomorrow?
你明天有时间吗？
Nǐ míngtiān yǒu shíjiān ma?
Do you have time tomorrow?
What time? (morning / afternoon)
上午九点 / 下午三点
shàngwǔ jiǔ diǎn / xiàwǔ sān diǎn
9 AM / 3 PM (use 点 diǎn for "o'clock")
Let's meet / see you then
明天见！/ 星期五见！
Míngtiān jiàn! / Xīngqīwǔ jiàn!
See you tomorrow! / See you Friday!

时间 (shíjiān) = time · 点 (diǎn) = o'clock · 见 (jiàn) = to see / meet. Combine them: 下午两点见 = "See you at 2 PM."

Dialogue 1 · Full

Tell me about your family.

A
你有兄弟姐妹吗？
Nǐ yǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi ma?
Do you have any siblings?
B
有！我有一个哥哥和一个妹妹。你呢？
Yǒu! Wǒ yǒu yī gè gēge hé yī gè mèimei. Nǐ ne?
Yes! I have one older brother and one younger sister. And you?
A
我没有兄弟姐妹。我是独生女。
Wǒ méiyǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi. Wǒ shì dúshēngnǚ.
I don't have any siblings. I'm an only child.
B
你爸爸妈妈做什么工作？
Nǐ bàba māma zuò shénme gōngzuò?
What do your parents do for work?
A
我爸爸是医生，我妈妈是老师。
Wǒ bàba shì yīshēng, wǒ māma shì lǎoshī.
My dad is a doctor, my mom is a teacher.
B
太好了！你有几个好朋友？
Tài hǎo le! Nǐ yǒu jǐ gè hǎo péngyou?
Awesome! How many close friends do you have?
A
我有很多朋友！我们都是同学。
Wǒ yǒu hěn duō péngyou! Wǒmen dōu shì tóngxué.
I have many friends! We are all classmates.

兄弟姐妹 (xiōngdì jiěmèi) = siblings (lit. "brothers and sisters") · 独生女/子 (dúshēngnǚ/zǐ) = only child · 都 (dōu) = all / both

Dialogue 2 · Full

Making plans.

A
你今天有时间吗？
Nǐ jīntiān yǒu shíjiān ma?
Do you have time today?
B
今天没有。明天可以吗？
Jīntiān méiyǒu. Míngtiān kěyǐ ma?
Not today. Is tomorrow okay?
A
明天可以！明天是星期几？
Míngtiān kěyǐ! Míngtiān shì xīngqī jǐ?
Tomorrow works! What day is tomorrow?
B
明天是星期三。上午十点怎么样？
Míngtiān shì xīngqīsān. Shàngwǔ shí diǎn zěnme yàng?
Tomorrow is Wednesday. How about 10 AM?
A
好！谢谢你！
Hǎo! Xièxie nǐ!
Great! Thank you!
B
不客气！星期三见！
Bú kèqi! Xīngqīsān jiàn!
You're welcome! See you Wednesday!

可以 (kěyǐ) = can / okay / may · 怎么样 (zěnme yàng) = how about? / what do you think? · 见 (jiàn) = see / meet
