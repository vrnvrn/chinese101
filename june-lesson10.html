June 10:
School, Work & Getting Around

Words 151–175 · Studying, working, and the verbs of movement

Lesson Goals

By the end of this lesson…

  • 1Talk about studying: 学, 学习, 上学, 上课, 下课
  • 2Talk about work: 上班, 下班, 工作, 做, 做饭
  • 3Use the core movement verbs: 来, 去, 回, 到
  • 4Say where you 坐 (sit), 住 (live), and stay
  • 5Handle errands: 买, 卖, 找, 穿, 开车, 打电话
  • 6Arrange to 见 (meet) someone
