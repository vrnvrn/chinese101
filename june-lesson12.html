Sentence Bank

ChinesePinyinEnglish
我哥哥是中学生。Wǒ gēge shì zhōngxuéshēng.My older brother is in middle school.
今天是五月一日。Jīntiān shì wǔ yuè yī rì.Today is May 1st.
明天我去学校。Míngtiān wǒ qù xuéxiào.Tomorrow I'm going to school.
上午我有课。Shàngwǔ wǒ yǒu kè.I have class in the morning.
现在三点。Xiànzài sān diǎn.It's three o'clock now.
他在睡。Tā zài shuì.He is sleeping.
我听歌。Wǒ tīng gē.I listen to music.
孩子们在玩。Háizi men zài wán.The kids are playing.
妈妈五点下班。Māma wǔ diǎn xiàbān.Mom gets off work at five.
我到学校了。Wǒ dào xuéxiào le.I've arrived at school.
我买了一本书。Wǒ mǎi le yì běn shū.I bought a book.
我在学校。Wǒ zài xuéxiào.I'm at school.
我喜欢喝茶。Wǒ xǐhuan hē chá.I like drinking tea.
我可以坐这儿吗？Wǒ kěyǐ zuò zhèr ma?May I sit here?
